Game Rules and Instructions


Player

Use the arrow keys, WASD or click with your mouse (one tile at a time) to move the player around the maze. If you're on mobile, tilt your device in the direction you wish to go in.

Keys

Collect all the keys to unlock the exit.

Collectibles

Collect as many collectibles as you can for bonus points.

Exit

Reach the exit after collecting all keys to complete the level.

Enemies

Avoid enemies as they will cause you to lose a life.

Traps

Watch out for traps that can hinder your progress.
