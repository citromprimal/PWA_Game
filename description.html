Game Rules and Instructions


Game Description

In this game, you are the Runner. You're trying to make your way out of the maze, but there are enemies and traps that will try to stop you. Use your skills to navigate through the maze and reach the exit.

Runner

Use the arrow keys, WASD or drag your mouse on the white tiles to move the player around the maze. If you're on mobile, tilt your device in the direction you wish to go in.

Keys

Collect all the keys to unlock the exit.

Collectibles

Collect as many collectibles as you can for bonus points.

Exit

Reach the exit after collecting all keys to complete the level.

Enemies

Avoid enemies as they will cause you to lose a life.

Traps

Watch out for traps that can hinder your progress.
